## Rotor Faults

### Classification

- in PMSM/BLDC:
  - [Demagnetization](../motor-fault/demagnetization.md)
  - permanent magnet cracks
- in IM:
  - bar damage
  - broken end ring
  - windings related faults
  - iron related faults
  - broken rotor bars

### Causes

- too many starts
- sudden action or outburst
- vibration
- physical damage
- excessive heat.
