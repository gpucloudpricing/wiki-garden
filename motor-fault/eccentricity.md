## Eccentricity

### Notes

- also called air-gap irregularities
- when air gap>10%

### Causes

- bad bearing
- worn bearing
- bent rotor shaft
- excessive load
- unequal air gap between rotor and stator

### Effects

- stray current in stator winding
- heat
- losses

### Classification

- static eccentricities
- dynamic eccentricities
- combined eccentricities
