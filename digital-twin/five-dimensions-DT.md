The dimensions according to [Falekas. 2021. *Digital twin in electrical machine control and predictive maintenance: state-of-the-art and future prospects*](zotero://select/items/1_S8ZD9NAZ) are : 
1. Physical; 
2. Virtual; 
3. Service; 
4. Data; 
5. Connections.
